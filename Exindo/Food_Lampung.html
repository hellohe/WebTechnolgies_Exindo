Keripik Pisang (Sweet Banana Chip)

Keripik pisang is the most familiar snack from Lampung. Everyone who has tried this snack was very delighted because delicacy that given is so great. There are so many tastes of this snack, such as original, strawberry, melon, cheese, chocolate, milk, coffee, etc.

Gabing

Gabing is the traditional cuisine made from coconut trunk. The young coconut trunk is cut with a medium-size after that it is boiled with coconut milk and vegetables. The trunk of this palm is sweet and savory taste.
