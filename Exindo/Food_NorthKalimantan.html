North Kalimantan

Soka Crab

vote data

In Tarakan City is famous for its seafood especially its crabs. The most popular type of crab is the Soka Crab. The whole body of this crab can be eaten. Soka Crab shells are soft and can be eaten. Soka Crabs can also be made snacks or crackers.

Kapah

vote data

Besides soka crab, Tarakan city also has other famous dish named Kapah. Kapah is a kind of shellfish in the coastal town of Tarakan. If visiting the Amal beach, Kapah akam is the signature dish and favorite foods for visitors. Kapah is served boiled and the chili sauce is the specialty for this dish. Almost all the restaurants there will provide Kapah.
