Banten

DEBUS (Banten Traditional Martial Art)

vote data

Of serang regency originally developed by Sultan Ageng Tirtayasa, one of famous sultan in Banten. Combination of traditional martial art performance and body invulnerability. The whole performance consist of Gembruk- the opening act with drumming percussion, Beluk- strident hysterical sound when it began to reach heavy level of performance and Pencak- single or pairs of traditional martial art performance.

Rampak Bedug

vote data

Rampak bedug is a group of dancers accompanied by music instruments, small bedug function as rhythm and dynamic, big bedug as bass and "shalawatan" as a melody. Usually presented in harvest time also performed as musical competition among villages.

Angklung Buhun

vote data

Buhun in sundanese language means OLD. Angklung buhun presented in ritual ceremony such as seren taun in cisungsang village. The traditional art has simply characteristic in lyric and song, creates comfortable atmosphere, peace and harmony.

Handycraft

vote data

Banten province has rich specific handycraft, spreading in every region. Taman jaya with wood carving handycraft, Bumi jaya much kind of pottery for decoration purpose, and rangkasbitung produce souvenir made of opal and onyx.
