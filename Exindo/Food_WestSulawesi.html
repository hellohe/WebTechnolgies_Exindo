Jepa

Jepa is a traditional bread of the Mandars ethnic group, made from a mix of Cassava and coconut and baked using traditional methods. This traditional Mandar bread has a plain taste and acts as a complementary dish to the main food, which is the Bau Tuing tuing and/or chopped squids.

Ikan Terbang

Flying fish, the locals refer to it as wink-wink fish, have a typical fin-shaped screen. This fish has a Latin name parexocoetus brachypterus. Fish belonging to the family exotidae has distinctive characteristics, namely fin large breasts that cause this kind of fish can glide through the air though briefly. Or perhaps more precisely is hovering over the water surface. The mechanism of this fly is actually a defense mechanism of the fish for himself from the pursuit of prey. Flying distance by which this occurred is usually not much, about 50s meters, but others cite the fact that this fish, by utilizing the power of ocean waves, can fly a distance of approximately 400 meters.

Culinary tour wink-wink fish or flying fish can be found if we pass through the Trans-Sulawesi, as we go through the Somba village, subdistrict Sendana, Majene (reading: maje 'ne') are included into the province of West Sulawesi.
