Sup Ikan Batam (Fish Soup)

Batam is known for its fresh seafood. One of the must-try seafood treats is the Batam fish soup. It is usually available in two options â€“ just fish or mixed seafood. The fillet used is from the snapper. Mixed seafood option comes with fish balls and shrimps. The clear soup has a strong and rich fish stock. Those that have no problem with this sort of aroma will find the soup very fresh and tasty.

Epok-epok (Curry Puff)

Epok-epok is a curry puff which can be found in many stores, it is smaller than curry puff. Other varieties of epok-epok are filled with a half boiled egg instead of chicken.

Otak-otak

Otak-otak is a cake made of fish meat and spices. It is widely known across Southeast Asia, where it is traditionally served fresh, wrapped inside a banana leaf. It is also available in stores as frozen food and even canned food.
