Kuncaran Mask Dance

vote data

Kuncaran Mask Dance, is a dance that tells about a king that being rejected by a girl he loved. This dance is is having strong yet delicate movements that showing the anger of the rejection but also the gentle heart of the king who love the girl. As mentioned, this dance is mainly using mask as the primary wardrobe that typical of West Java mask character. Moreover, the dancers are also wore traditional Javanese clothes. Behind the mask, it can be men or women who wear a figure of mask.

Merak Dance

vote data

Merak dance is a traditional folk dance originating from Pasundan land, West Java. This dance was created by Raden Tjetjep Somantri in 1950s and it had been rearrange by Irawati Durban in 1965. Merak in English means Peacock bird. From its name, it clearly describes the dance that symbolize the elegant of peacock movements.

Merak dance is can be performed solo or in groups. The dancers of this beautiful dance are wearing scarf that perform the tail of the peacock with multicolor scenic tone. Moreover, the dancers are also wearing the crown a la peacock head crown. Merak dance is having delicate hand movements and it follows by traditional ‘Gamelan’ music, that becomes the main characteristic of this dance. Each movement in this dance is meaningful and happy, that is why it sometime becomes the welcome dance to greet the guest of honor in wedding ceremony.

Kecapi Suling

vote data

Kecapi Suling , Sundanese Culture Harmonious Society indeed have known stringed instrument . Melody and beautiful harmony created from the plucked instrument . However , not many people who know about the lute or lyre flute which is a unique art Cianjur city . In essence , consists of flute harp lute ovary . Ovarian harp shaped like a boat , so it is better known as a harp lute boat . This art is usually presented instrumentally , but not infrequently also lute flute used to accompany chanting songs from sekar interpreter . The songs are presented as gamelan , Talutur , SINOM , Kaleon using Salendro barrel , Sorog , or Pelog .
