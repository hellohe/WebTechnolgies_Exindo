Jambi

Panggang Ikan Mas

vote data

Roasted goldfish is one of the signature dishes in Jambi. The gold fish is dipped in lime and salt for 5 minutes and mix with coconut milk and spices for the seasoning. Smear the fish with the mixture and roast it while the coconut milk is sprinkled to the fish with lemongrass.

Gelamai Perentak is a snack from Jambi, Sumatra. It is made from glutinous rice, sugar and coconut.

Bolu Kojo

Kojo is a typical cake confectionary area of South Sumatra and the surrounding areas, especially in the area of Jambi, Riau, Bengkulu, and Palembang. This cake is usually served at traditional events.
