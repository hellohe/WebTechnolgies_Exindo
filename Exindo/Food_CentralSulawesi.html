Central Sulawesi

Kaledo

Calf foot Donggala, better known by the name this is a typical food Kaledo Donggala society. Located in the province of Central Sulawesi, in the city of Palu. The food is similar tooxtail soup , the difference between the bones of the feet of the ox and served with rice but not with potatoes. Bone itself is a vertebra that was filled with knee marrow. There is also a saying, that Kaledo comes from Kaili language, language Palu residents. Ka means Hard, and Not Ledo means, so it can be interpreted as "not hard".

Sayur Kelor

It is one of the signature dish in Palu which liked by many people in Central Sulawesi. To make it, cut and clean the kelor leaves. Boil some water and put red onion, kunci, kelor leaves and salt. After it is cooked, add some tomatoes.

Ikan

Palumara fish is delicious snacks are made from fish, how to cook fish palumara fairly simple with easy ingredients on the market to the archipelago, which made him try this recipe mw palumara fish. Siangi milkfish then cut each fish into 3 pieces, wash and drain. Marinate the fish with lime juice and salt, leave 20 minutes. Sautee the garlic until fragrant. Add onion and red pepper, stirring until wilted. Add tamarind water and water, let it boil. Put the piece of fish. Add kaffir lime leaves, cayenne pepper, and brown sugar flavor. Cook until done. Enter the lemon juice, stir briefly, and remove from heat. Palumara Serve with a sprinkling of fried onions.
